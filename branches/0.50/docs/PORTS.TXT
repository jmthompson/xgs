The directories under arch/ contain code specific to a particular port
of XGS. Currently the following ports are available:

arch/generic	Contains the generic sound driver; used by any port
		that doesn't have native sound support.

		Maintainer: Joshua M. Thompson (invid@optera.com)
		    Status: Available

arch/oss	Open Sound System sound driver.

		Maintainer: Joshua M. Thompson (invid@optera.com)
		    Status: Available

arch/x11	X11 display and input drivers.

		Maintainer: Joshua M. Thompson (invid@optera.com)
		    Status: Available

arch/beos	BeOS display, input, and sound drivers.

		Maintainer: Eric Shepard (sheppy@logicware.tstonramp.com)
		    Status: In progress

arch/warp	OS/2 Warp display, input, and sound drivers.

		Maintainer: Tschopp Gilles (tschopp@icare.ch)
		    Status: In progress

arch/win32	Win32 display, input, and sound drivers.

		Maintainer: David Andrew Ross (snakey@pobox.com)
		    Status: Available

arch/macos	MacOS display, input, and sound drivers.

		Maintainer: Michael Bytnar (m-bytnar@uiuc.edu)
		    Status: Available separately in binary form only

arch/msdos	MS-DOS display, input, and sound drivers.

		Maintainer: Tschopp Gilles (tschopp@icare.ch)
		    Status: In progress

arch/svgalib	SVGAlib display and input drivers.

		Maintainer: Joshua M. Thompson (invid@optera.com)
		    Status: In progress
